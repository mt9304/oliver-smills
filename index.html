About Oliver Smills

Oliver Smills is a children's book author who delights in crafting stories that entertain and engage young minds. His collection spans from imaginative storybooks to rhythmic rhyming tales. Residing in British Columbia, Canada, Smills leads a fullfilling life as a full-time software developer and a dedicated author in his spare time. Outside of literature, Oliver finds joy in spending quality moments with pets — both his own and those of friends — and indulging in the challenges of indoor rock climbing. Oliver Smills' commitment to crafting imaginative tales and fostering a love for reading among young audiences remains at the heart of his writing journey.
